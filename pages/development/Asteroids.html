Asteroids: The Pollinator Edition

What is Asteroids?

This version of asteroids fun and new take on the classic video game, Asteroids. Instead of bullets shot at asteroids in space by a spaceship, the player floats around as a beehive and shoots bees at flowers. It was created in the spring of 2018 for the course Interactive Medial Development.

Tools

Created in Unity, coded in C#

Controls:

  • Left and right arrow keys control hive rotation.
  • Up arrow controls hive acceleration.
  • Space bar shoots bees at flowers

Features

  • Invincibility mode: allows the user to take no damage and position themselves in preparation for the game. Bullets have no effect when in this mode.
  • Ranking: a ranking system displays the players rank according to their score.
  • Delay: there is a delay in asteroid effectiveness when the game starts up. This is done for a similar reason as invincibility mode.
