PetalPedia

What is PetalPedia?

PetalPedia is an API-based app for flowers that helps users get all the pertinent info they may need when starting to grow said flower/s. It includes information such as soil moisture, amount of light needed, and what zones the plants will grow best in. The API keeps track of the flowers in question. Users can add their own flowers or scroll through the pre-loaded list.
